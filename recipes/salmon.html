Baked Salmon

Description

The go to dinner meal. Quick and easy and always delicious. Serve with a side of rice, thought you're on your own when it comes to cooking the rice.

Ingredients

  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Onion powder
  • Old Bay seasoning
  • Butter
  • Chopped garlic (Alternative: garlic powder)
  • Your chosen piece of salmon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (204 degrees C)
  2. Add butter (a quarter for each piece of salmon depending on size) and 2 teaspoons of chopped garlic into a bowl and sprinkle old bay into the mix
  3. Microwave the mix for 45 seconds
  4. Place salmon piece(s) into a pan with tinfoil
  5. Season the salmon with salt then pepper
  6. Season the salmon with onion powder then old bay
  7. Take your hand and rub the seasoning everywhere (don't forget the sides)
  8. Take the melted butter mix and stir it up
  9. Pour that melted butter mix all over the salmon
  10. Put the dish into the oven
  11. Check in 15 minutes and then 5 every 5 minutes until salmon is finished
  12. Clean up and enjoy!
